Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I log in to the system? ‚ñº
Use your university email address and password to log in. If you're a student, use your student email (student@charusat.edu.in). Faculty and admin users should use their respective credentials.
What are the different user roles? ‚ñº
There are three user roles: Students can browse and register for events, Faculty can create events and manage registrations, and Admins can approve/reject events and manage the entire system.

Event Management

How do I register for an event? ‚ñº
Browse the event list, click on an event to view details, and click the "Register" button. You'll receive a confirmation message once registered. You can view all your registered events on your dashboard.
Can I cancel my event registration? ‚ñº
Yes, you can cancel your registration from the event details page or from your dashboard's "My Registered Events" section. Click the "Cancel Registration" button to unregister.
How do I create an event? (Faculty) ‚ñº
Faculty members can create events from their dashboard by clicking "Create Event". Fill in the event details including title, category, date, time, venue, and description. The event will be sent to admin for approval before it becomes visible to students.
What happens after I create an event? ‚ñº
After creation, your event enters "Pending" status and is sent to the admin for review. The admin will either approve or reject it. If approved, the event becomes visible to students for registration. If rejected, you'll see the rejection reason on your dashboard.

Account & Login Issues

I forgot my password. What should I do? ‚ñº
Contact the IT helpdesk at support@charusat.edu.in or call +91-2697-265011 for password reset assistance. For this demo, use the provided demo credentials.
Can I change my profile information? ‚ñº
Profile editing is currently not available in this demo version. In the full system, you would be able to update your information from the Profile page.
